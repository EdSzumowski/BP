6450 – Theft of Services or Property

6450 – Theft of Services or Property Share to Facebook Share to Twitter Share to LinkedIn Share to Pinterest Share to Email Print this page Post RSS Feeds 6450 – Theft of Services or Property June 21 , 2023 The theft of services or property from the District by an employee will result in immediate disciplinary action that can lead to dismissal or other penalty, and shall not preclude the filing of criminal or civil charges by the District.
