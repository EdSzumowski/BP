4321 – Planning and Evaluation of District Programming and Operations Share to Facebook Share to Twitter Share to LinkedIn Share to Pinterest Share to Email Print this page Post RSS Feeds 4321 – Planning and Evaluation of District Programming and Operations June 20 , 2023 The Superintendent is charged with developing an annual report on the programming and operations of the District minimally, the report will include: Five-year student enrollment projections. Presentation and analysis of Profiles of Performance. Included in this report should be: District accountability status; Performance data on 3 through 8 state assessments, Regents exams, and SWD; Graduation rate data; Individual student drop-out. Three-year program projections. Two-year projection of transportation, operation and maintenance needs. Two-year expenditure projections. This report will be submitted to the Board on an annual basis which will permit sufficient time for review and discussion to impact program improvement and budget development processes.
