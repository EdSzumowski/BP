6460 – Jury Duty

6460 – Jury Duty Share to Facebook Share to Twitter Share to LinkedIn Share to Pinterest Share to Email Print this page Post RSS Feeds 6460 – Jury Duty June 21 , 2023 A District employee called for jury duty shall receive his/her full day’s pay from the School District plus mileage from the State. No employee shall be entitled to receive the per diem allowance from the Unified Court System for any regularly scheduled workday on which jury duty is rendered if on such a day his/her wages are not withheld on account of such service. References Judiciary Law Section 521(b)
