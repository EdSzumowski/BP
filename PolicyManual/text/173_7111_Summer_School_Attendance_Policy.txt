7111 – Summer School Attendance Policy

7111 – Summer School Attendance Policy Share to Facebook Share to Twitter Share to LinkedIn Share to Pinterest Share to Email Print this page Post RSS Feeds 7111 – Summer School Attendance Policy March 20 , 2017 The following is the attendance policy for the operation of summer school programming in the Broadalbin-Perth Central School District. Students in summer enrichment programs at the Middle School in grades 6 through 8 will be allowed a maximum number of absences as follows: If the summer enrichment program is held four (4) days per week, a maximum of two (2) absences will be allowed for the entirety of the summer program. If the summer enrichment program is held five (5) days per week, a maximum of three (3) absences will be allowed for the entirety of the summer program. All 9 through 12 grade students attending the HFM BOCES Regional Summer School are expected to follow the attendance policy of the regional school.
