2340 – Board Self-Evaluation

2340 – Board Self-Evaluation Share to Facebook Share to Twitter Share to LinkedIn Share to Pinterest Share to Email Print this page Post RSS Feeds 2340 – Board Self-Evaluation January 1 , 2014 The Board shall review the effectiveness of its internal operations at least once annually and will formulate a plan for improving its performance. The Superintendent and others who work regularly with the Board may be asked to participate in this review and to suggest ways by which the Board can improve its functioning as a legislative body. section2
