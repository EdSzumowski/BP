5620R – Capitalization Policy of Assets

5620R – Capitalization Policy of Assets Share to Facebook Share to Twitter Share to LinkedIn Share to Pinterest Share to Email Print this page Post RSS Feeds 5620R – Capitalization Policy of Assets January 28 , 2019 The Broadalbin-Perth Central School District Board of Education hereby establishes the following Capitalization Policy of Assets: Any Single Capital Asset purchase of $5,000 Five Thousand dollars or more The dollar values set by the policy will not affect the District’s existing physical inventory procedures. Assets with value at or above the policy limits will be tracked in the physical inventory of capital assets. Assets with value below the policy limits will be tracked using the District’s inventory tracking procedures for non-capital assets.
