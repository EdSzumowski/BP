# Venue Inquiry Email and Questions

## Sample inquiry email

Subject: Spring 2027 retirement recognition event inquiry - 100 to 200 guests

Hello,

I am helping plan an invitation-based retirement recognition event for a longtime school district superintendent in spring 2027. We are looking for a dignified, warm, adult-focused venue suitable for a school-community celebration.

We are comparing two possible formats:

1. Buffet-style dinner
2. Cocktail-style reception with substantial hors d'oeuvres or food stations

Estimated attendance may range from 100 to 200 guests. We prefer a Thursday, Friday, or Saturday evening, but would consider Sunday or another day if pricing or availability is meaningfully better.

The event will include a short formal program with speakers, and possibly a slideshow or video tribute. We would need appropriate room setup, microphone, podium or lectern if available, and screen/projector or other basic A/V support.

Important note on alcohol: district funds cannot be used to pay for alcohol. If adult beverages are available, please quote food, room, non-alcoholic beverages, service, tax, staffing, and A/V separately from any alcohol charges. We are interested only in arrangements such as cash bar, guest-paid consumption bar, beer/wine-only cash bar, or privately/non-district-funded drink tickets if permitted.

Could you please provide the following information?

- Available Thursday, Friday, Saturday, and Sunday dates in March, April, or May 2027
- Maximum seated capacity and maximum reception/cocktail capacity
- Buffet dinner menu options and estimated per-person pricing
- Heavy hors d'oeuvres / cocktail reception options and estimated per-person pricing
- Room rental fees, food and beverage minimums, service charges, staffing charges, taxes, gratuity policy, and deposit requirements
- Whether alcohol can be handled as guest-paid or separately billed so no district funds pay for alcohol
- Whether outside catering is allowed, if applicable
- Whether microphone, podium, screen/projector, Wi-Fi, and video playback are available
- Parking and accessibility notes
- Contract terms, cancellation terms, and insurance requirements

Thank you. We are preparing a comparison table and would appreciate any package materials or sample menus you can share.

Sincerely,

[Name]
[Role]
[Phone]
[Email]

## Questions to ask each venue

### Capacity and layout

- What is the comfortable seated capacity for a buffet dinner with a short program?
- What is the comfortable reception capacity with high-top tables, food stations, and enough seating for older guests?
- Can all guests see and hear speakers from the main room?
- Is there a natural place for a podium, screen, or small stage?
- Can the room be set to avoid making the bar the focal point?

### Date and timing

- Which Thursday, Friday, Saturday, or Sunday dates are available in March, April, and May 2027?
- Are Thursday or Sunday events priced differently than Friday or Saturday?
- How many hours are included?
- What are the earliest setup time and latest event end time?

### Food and non-alcoholic beverages

- What buffet packages are available for 100, 150, and 200 guests?
- What heavy hors d'oeuvres or station-style packages are available for 100, 150, and 200 guests?
- Are coffee, tea, water, and soft drinks included?
- Are dessert, cake service, or passed appetizers included?
- Can menus be customized for a school-community retirement event rather than a wedding?
- Are vegetarian, gluten-free, and allergy-sensitive options available?

### Alcohol separation

- Can the event have a cash bar paid directly by guests?
- Can alcohol be invoiced separately to a private/non-district host?
- Can drink tickets be billed separately to a non-district source?
- Is beer/wine-only service available?
- Are bartender fees, minimums, or bar setup charges required even for cash bar?
- Can the final district-facing invoice exclude all alcohol charges?

### A/V and program

- Is a wireless microphone included?
- Is a podium or lectern available?
- Is a projector and screen available? If not, can one be brought in?
- Can the venue play a video tribute with sound?
- Is there Wi-Fi and a hardwired connection if needed?
- Is there a stage, riser, or clear speaking area?
- Is light background music or live acoustic music permitted?

### Costs and contract

- What is the per-person quote for buffet dinner at 100, 150, and 200 guests?
- What is the per-person quote for heavy hors d'oeuvres/stations at 100, 150, and 200 guests?
- Is there a room rental fee?
- Is there a food and beverage minimum?
- What service charge, administrative fee, gratuity, and tax apply?
- What deposit is required and when?
- What is the cancellation policy?
- What insurance or certificate requirements apply?
- Are prices guaranteed for spring 2027? If not, what escalation should be assumed?

### Accessibility and logistics

- How many parking spaces are available?
- Is the room wheelchair accessible?
- Are restrooms accessible and near the event room?
- Is there a covered drop-off area?
- Is coat check available or needed?
- Are directional signs allowed?
- Is there a private room for family/speakers before the program?

## Suggested scoring criteria

Score each venue 1 to 5:

- Distance/travel convenience
- Fit with dignified school-community tone
- Capacity for 150-200
- Program/A/V quality
- Food quality and menu fit
- Cost/value
- Alcohol separation simplicity
- Parking and accessibility
- Contract simplicity
- Overall confidence
