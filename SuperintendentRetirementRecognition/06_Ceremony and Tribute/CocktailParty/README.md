# Cocktail Party Venue Research Packet

Purpose: planning materials for an invitation-based retirement celebration for Stephen Tomlinson in spring 2027.

Important limitation: live web browsing was not available when this packet was prepared. The files therefore distinguish between planning estimates and items requiring direct venue confirmation. Do not treat any package price, capacity, bar arrangement, or availability note as confirmed until a venue responds in writing.

Files in this folder:

- `01_Venue_Research_Memo.md` - working venue research memo, shortlist, budget ranges, alcohol-structure notes, and next-step process.
- `02_Venue_Comparison_Table.csv` - comparison table for candidate venues.
- `03_Venue_Inquiry_Tracker.csv` - tracker template for outreach and responses.
- `04_Inquiry_Email_and_Questions.md` - sample inquiry email plus questions to ask each venue.
- `05_Budget_Range_Model.csv` - first-pass budget model for 100, 150, and 200 guests.

Recommended use:

1. Send inquiries to the recommended shortlist first.
2. Record every response in the tracker.
3. Replace planning estimates with written venue quotes.
4. Keep alcohol costs separate from district-paid food, room, A/V, and non-alcoholic beverage charges.
5. Re-rank venues after written quotes are returned.
